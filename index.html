At Rainbow High School (Pride of Liechtenstein) we PRIDE ourselves in our organizational skills.
How else do you think we manage over 10,000 students on our campus everyday?

This website's purpose is to benefit our hardworking yearbook team and assist them in their annual process of the highly anticipated yearbook.

Frequently Asked Questions:

  • What is the 'Students' Page for? The 'Students' page is meant to display student data and holds information such as student IDs and their first and last name.
  • What is the 'Graduating Classes' Page for? The 'Graduating Classes' page is meant to display information for each graduation year and display which pages of the yearbook each grade-level has their photos on.
  • What is the 'Clubs' Page for? The 'Clubs' page is meant to display basic information for each one of our many clubs such as clubID and club name.
  • What is the 'Sports' Page for? The 'Sports' page is meant to display basic information for each sport that we offer. This information includes sportID, which sport it is, the season and the varsity level.
  • What is the 'Sports' Page for? The 'Events' page is meant to display information regarding our many highly anticipated events throughout the year. Information you'll find here includes eventID, the name of the event, and the event date.
  • What is the 'Club Memberships' Page for? The 'Club Memberships' page displays information about students who are in clubs.
    For each club that a student joins, there is an entry in our database displaying which club they joined, their role in the club, and an associated page number in the yearbook where they have a photo.
  • What is the 'Sports Memberships' Page for? The 'Sports Memberships' page is meant to serve a similar purpose as the club membership page.
    For each sport that a student joins, there is an entry in our database displaying which sport they participate in, their role in the sport and an associated page number in the yearbook where they have a photo.
  • What is the 'Sports Memberships' Page for? The 'Event Memberships' page also has a similar purpose to the other membership pages.
    For each event, we keep track of students and their attendance of events, their role in the event, and a page number with their associated event photos.
